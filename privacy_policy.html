Privacy Policy
EFFECTIVE DATE: FEBRUARY 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Tarot Oracle ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your data. Our commitment is to provide a mystical and insightful experience while respecting your absolute right to privacy.

1. Zero Data Collection

We believe your spiritual journey is personal. Tarot Oracle does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account registration, names, email addresses, or phone numbers to access our Service.

2. AI Processing (Gemini)

To provide personalized tarot interpretations, our App utilizes advanced Generative AI (Google Gemini). When you interact with the oracle:

  • Your specific questions and card selections are sent securely to the AI provider to generate the reading.
  • No personal identity or device-specific identifiers are attached to these requests.
  • The AI provider does not receive information that could link your questions back to you as an individual.

3. Local Device Storage

For your convenience, some data is stored exclusively on your physical device:

  • Reading History: Your past readings are saved locally so you can revisit them.
  • User Preferences: Settings like language selection, background music, and sound effect toggles.

This data never leaves your device and is permanently erased if you choose to uninstall the application.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses essential third-party services that may collect anonymized technical data for functional purposes:

  • Google Play Services (for app stability and updates)
  • Google AdMob (for non-personalized advertising, if applicable)

5. Children's Sanctity

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly process data from minors, as we do not collect data from any user regardless of age.

6. Policy Evolutions

We may refine this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any updates will be published here with an updated "Effective Date". Your continued use of the App signifies acceptance of these terms.
